Bottom Line

CCW has two distinct buyer populations. Summer buyers (Aug–Sep) are high-volume, price-sensitive, and seasonal — the Sep price hike at peak demand cost the platform its best retention window, dragging delta to −9.5pp. Winter buyers (Nov–Jan) are low-volume but remarkably loyal — a price cut on 1pc or Combo brings them in and they stay, lifting delta to +5.8pp. The strategy implication is clear: hold prices through Aug–Sep to protect the conversion window; use targeted Combo cuts in winter to activate the loyal core.
